About Prepora

We're on a mission to make healthy eating simple, enjoyable, and accessible for everyone.

Our Story

Prepora was born from a simple frustration that we all know too well: the daily struggle of deciding what to eat. After countless evenings staring at the refrigerator wondering what to cook, and too many last-minute takeout orders, we knew there had to be a better way.

We envisioned an app that could understand what ingredients you have on hand, respect your dietary preferences, and instantly generate delicious meal plans complete with step-by-step recipes. With the power of AI, we turned that vision into reality.

🎯 What We Believe

We believe that eating well shouldn't be complicated. Whether you're a busy professional, a parent juggling multiple responsibilities, or someone just starting their cooking journey, everyone deserves access to simple, personalized meal planning.

Good food brings people together, nourishes our bodies, and brightens our days. Prepora is designed to remove the stress of meal planning so you can focus on what really matters - enjoying the food you eat and the moments you share.

💡 The Idea Behind Prepora

The name "Prepora" comes from "Prepare" - because we're here to help you prepare smarter. Our AI-powered technology analyzes your available ingredients and preferences to create personalized meal plans in seconds.

Whether you're craving something fresh and healthy or seeking the comfort of a hearty meal, Prepora adapts to your mood. Plan for a single day or an entire week - it's flexible meal planning that fits your lifestyle, not the other way around.
